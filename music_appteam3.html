SongStorm

About

SongStorm™ is an application for the iPad whose goal is to make it simple and intuitive
for aspiring songwriters as well as professional composers to create a product that looks like
classic sheet music, but offers a stylish interface that is fun for everyone to use. Are you tired
of having to write notes with a pen or pencil onto a piece of paper with a staff? You do not have to
look any further once you use this application because it offers a staff and notes as all composers are
used to, and you do not have to draw out the notes everytime you want to add them to the staff. SongStorm
has a number of features that make it a pleasure for any songwriter to use.

Features

To make it easy for users to add notes to the staff, there are buttons from which you can choose the type
of note you want to add. The choices include quarter notes, whole notes, half notes among others. Once you have
selected the type of note you want to add, it is also very simple to set what pitch you want the note to have - C, D, E
etc. Once a note is added to the staff and the pitch is selected, the app plays the pitch you have added so that you can hear
how your composition would currently sound if it were to be played.
